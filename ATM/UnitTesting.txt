Unit testing is a software development process where one tests individual parts of codes, like methods, to make sure it works as expected. 
UT is very important to make sure the foundation of low-level coding is correct and reliable. Esepcially with bigger projects, 
programmers want to know that their foundation code is dependable and future bugs are less likely to occur from it. 
It also helps find early bugs and solidifies less problematic situation later on.
